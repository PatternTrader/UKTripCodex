      Ideas of Things to Do

      💡 Ideas

      White Cliffs of Dover

      Coastal add-on — pair with Stonehenge day or standalone
      • Take the train from London St Pancras International to Dover Priory (~1h 20m). National Rail London – Dover.
      • Walk the White Cliffs of Dover trail for classic chalk-cliff views over the English Channel.
      • Optional: visit Dover Castle and its wartime tunnels.
      • Return to London by train for the evening.
      💡 Ideas

      Stratford-upon-Avon Day Trip

      Displaced from April 6 — find it a new home!
      • Travel no-car to Bletchley Park by train from London Euston. National Rail London – Bletchley planning.
      • Guided no-car option for Stratford-upon-Avon: book coach + guide from London. Evan Evans Stratford day tour.
      • Tour the Royal Shakespeare Theatre.
      • Stop by Anne Hathaway's Cottage.
      • Take a River Avon walk.
      • Return to London by rail/coach — dinner at Rock & Sole Plaice (famous fish and chips near Covent Garden).
      💡 Ideas

      Edinburgh Castle & Old Town

      Displaced from April 7
      • Visit Edinburgh Castle in the morning.
      • Walk the Royal Mile toward Holyrood Palace.
      • Optional hike up Arthur's Seat.
      • Dinner in Old Town.
      💡 Ideas

      Stirling Day Trip

      Displaced from April 8
      • Day trip to Stirling Castle.
      • See the Wallace Monument area.
      • Visit the Bannockburn Heritage Centre.
      • Return to Edinburgh for the evening.
      💡 Ideas

      Highlands Drive

      Displaced from April 9
      • No-car guided option: book a 2-day Highlands + Inverness tour from Edinburgh. Timberbush 2-day Highlands/Inverness tour.
      • Visit Loch Ness and Urquhart Castle.
      • Continue through Glencoe Valley.
      • Overnight in Inverness or Fort William area.
      💡 Ideas

      Skye Morning & Return to London

      Displaced from April 10
      • No-car guided option for Skye + Eilean Donan from Edinburgh. Rabbie's 3-day Skye tour.
      • Quick scenic stop at Old Man of Storr.
      • Return toward Edinburgh on guided tour transport, then connect by evening train.
      • Take an evening LNER train from Edinburgh to London.
      💡 Ideas

      Rabbies Tour Highlights — Visit Independently

      These sites are covered by the Rabbies 3-day tour (Apr 7–9). Listed here if you ever want to revisit them self-guided.
      • Stirling Castle (Day 6) — Paid admission via Historic Environment Scotland. Book tickets.
      • Eilean Donan Castle (Days 6 & 8) — Exterior viewpoints free; interior entry paid. Eilean Donan tickets.
      • Glencoe (Day 6) — Roadside viewpoints free; some car parks and NTS facilities are paid.
      • Old Man of Storr (Day 7) — Trail access free; car park at the trailhead is commonly paid.
      • Dunvegan Castle (Day 7) — Paid admission (castle and gardens). Dunvegan Castle tickets.
      • Urquhart Castle / Loch Ness (Day 8) — Paid admission via Historic Environment Scotland. Urquhart Castle tickets.
      💡 Ideas

      Forbidden Planet London Megastore

      Dropped from itinerary — worth a visit if you have a free hour
      • Flagship pop-culture megastore at 179 Shaftesbury Ave — comics, manga, collectibles, sci-fi merch.
      • Right next to the British Museum area — easy to combine on a morning you're already in Bloomsbury.
      • Free entry — just a retail stop, no booking needed.
      • Forbidden Planet London store info.
